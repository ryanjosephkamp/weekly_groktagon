# Final Blog Post Synthesis Prompt

## Scope

Synthesize the eight completed weekly reports into one final Markdown post using the final blog post template.

## Requirements

- Use exactly eight main sections, one for each report.
- Use original wording.
- Ensure every factual claim is supported by the cited report material or its underlying source references.
- Include a clickable table of contents.
- Include a short introduction, conclusion, links section, and `## References` section.
- Do not include raw scrape output.
- Do not add unsupported claims.

## Required Section Order

1. Official xAI Sources Summary
2. Additional Web Deep Dive
3. X Platform Deep Dive
4. Research Platforms & Academic Deep Dive
5. Community & Ecosystem Insights
6. Competitor & Industry Context
7. Practical Tutorials & Vibe-Coding Spotlight
8. Forward-Looking Signals & Roadmap Synthesis
