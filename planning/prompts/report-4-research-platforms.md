# Report 4 Prompt — Research Platforms & Academic Deep Dive

## Scope

Create Report 4 from research papers, pre-prints, and academic discussions that directly reference the weekly topic or have clear implications for it.

## Requirements

- Separate direct references from broader technical implications.
- Use original wording.
- Cite every factual claim with paper links, DOI links, repository links, or database records.
- Avoid overstating findings beyond what the cited source supports.
- End with a `## References` section.

## Output Structure

1. Title
2. Short overview
3. Directly relevant research
4. Broader technical implications
5. Limitations and confidence notes
6. `## References`
