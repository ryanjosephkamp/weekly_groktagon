# Report 6 Prompt — Competitor & Industry Context

## Scope

Create Report 6 by placing the week’s developments in broader AI and LLM industry context.

## Requirements

- Compare only against cited, relevant industry developments from the target week.
- Use balanced language and original wording.
- Cite every factual claim with a source URL.
- Do not make unsupported competitive claims.
- Explain why each industry item matters to the weekly topic.
- End with a `## References` section.

## Output Structure

1. Title
2. Short overview
3. Relevant competitor or industry developments
4. Comparative context
5. Caveats and non-comparisons
6. `## References`
