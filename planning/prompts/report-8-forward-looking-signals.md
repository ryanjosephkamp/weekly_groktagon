# Report 8 Prompt — Forward-Looking Signals & Roadmap Synthesis

## Scope

Create Report 8 from credible forward-looking signals, indirect roadmap evidence, and strategic statements from reliable sources.

## Requirements

- Include only signals grounded in cited sources.
- Clearly separate confirmed statements from interpretation.
- Use original wording.
- Cite every factual claim with a source URL.
- Avoid predictions that are not supported by evidence.
- End with a `## References` section.

## Output Structure

1. Title
2. Short overview
3. Confirmed forward-looking statements
4. Indirect signals
5. What remains uncertain
6. `## References`
